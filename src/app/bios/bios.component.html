Our Mission

What We Do

Our primary focus is to improve city government services and operations that will ensure Worcester is well situated to address the needs of its residents, businesses, and visitors.

Why We Do It

Economic development and civic projects require that city government and departments are able to keep pace with the changing needs of its residents and businesses.

How We Do It

By implementing smart city solutions guided by data analysis, process improvement methodologies, and targeted technology platforms focused on scaling city services and operations scale as needed.
