Technology Platforms

Informing Worcester - The City's Open Data Portal

Informing Worcester is the City's new open data portal. Functional transparency is a primary focus for Informing Worcester; however, it is also intended to become a platform upon which to showcase the City's data driven approach to meeting its challenges and strategic goals.

GovQA - Public Records Request Portal

GovQA is an online public records request portal where the public can request municipal records maintained by the City of Worcester. It provides users with easy, fast and secure accessibility to public records. GovQA complements the City of Worcester's focus on transparency.

Policy and Research

Open Data Policy

Worcester recognizes Open Government as a key means for enabling public participation and effective government. The City is committed to expanding the data available to the public and providing the tools necessary for understanding and interpreting it.

City of Worcester Strategic Plan

The City of Worcester Strategic Plan, developed and launched in fiscal year 2020 after public input through formatted focus groups and a City-wide survey, is a key complement to the City's future Master Plan and the Worcester Public Schools' Strategic Plan.
