Open Data Portal

City of Worcester, MA

The City of Worcester is committed to transparency in government and public operations. In 2019, the City launched the online portal we call "Informing Worcester" to serve as its primary platform for free and easy access to public data.

Government Transparency through Data Access

The City of Worcester firmly believes that access to public data should be readily available to anyone who wants it. Launched in 2019, the city's open data portal has provided an easy to use platform for those needing access to available records.
